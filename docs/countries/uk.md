# United Kingdom

## Resources

The UK has a well-documented path to contracting. See this comprehensive community guide:

<https://github.com/tadast/switching-to-contracting-uk/>

This guide covers:
- Setting up a limited company
- IR35 regulations
- Accounting and taxes
- Insurance requirements
- Finding contracts

## Accounting Software

[FreeAgent](https://www.freeagent.com/) is recommended for UK freelancers and contractors:
- Designed for UK tax requirements
- Integrates with UK banks
- Handles VAT returns and Self Assessment
- Corporation Tax support for limited companies
